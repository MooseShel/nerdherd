# Nerd Herd - User Manual

## Welcome to Nerd Herd 1.0!
Connect with peers, find study spots, and book tutors in real-time.

### Navigation
- **Pinch to Zoom**: Explore the map with standard gestures.
- **Recenter**: Tap the GPS icon to snap back to your location.
- **Filters**: Use the top filter bar to toggle between `All`, `Tutors`, `Students`, or `Generic` spots.

### Study Modes
- **Ghost Mode**: Your location is hidden. You can see others, but they can't see you.
- **Study Mode**: Broadcast your approximate location to friends and peers for collaboration.

## üìÖ Scheduling & bookings
### Booking a Tutor
1. Tap on a Tutor's avatar on the map.
2. Tap **Book Session**.
3. Select a time and duration.
4. Confirm payment (Process is simulated for this version).

### Managing Appointments
- Go to your **Profile** > **My Schedule**.
- **Cancel**: You can cancel pending requests.
- **Reschedule**: Propose a new time for confirmed appointments.
- **Review**: Leave a star rating and comment after a session is completed.

## üõ°Ô∏è Admin Portal (Authorized Users Only)
Access via the **Admin Panel** button in the Settings menu.

### Features
- **Dashboard**: View high-level stats (Total Users, Active Appointments).
- **Moderation**: Review reported users and ban/unban accounts.
- **Ledger**: Track all platform transactions (Deposits, Payments, Refunds).
- **Support**: Manage support tickets submitted by users.
- **Verification**: Review and approve Tutor applications.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Settings & Customization
- **App Theme**: customize the look of the app. Choose between **Light Mode**, **Dark Mode**, or **System Default**.
- **Ghost Mode**: Toggle this to hide your location from the map while still being able to see others.
- **Push Notifications**: meaningful controls to enable or disable alerts for requests and messages.

## üí¨ Chat & Social
- **Peer Messaging**: Tap "Connect" on a user's profile. Once accepted, you can chat.
