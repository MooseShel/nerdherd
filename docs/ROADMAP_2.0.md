# Nerd Herd 2.0 Roadmap: The AI Evolution

## üåü Vision
To transform Nerd Herd from a location-based social app into an **intelligent, localized campus companion** that fosters genuine connections and academic success through AI-driven insights, without replacing the human element.

---

## Phase 1: The "Nerd Match" (Social & Connection)
*Focus: Connecting the right people at the right time.*

### 1. AI Study Buddy Matchmaking ("Nerd Match")
Instead of just filtering by subject, use AI to find compatible peers.
- **How it works**: Analyzes user bio, past study times, and course load.
- **The "Vibe" Score**: Matches based on study style (Silent vs. Social, Morning vs. Night).
- **Icebreakers**: AI suggests context-aware conversation starters upon connection (e.g., *"You both study regularly at the Main Library, maybe meet there?"*).

### 2. Smart "Vibe Check" for Spots
Make study spots feel alive.
- **Real-time Summaries**: "Library 3rd Floor is currently **Quiet** and **80% full**." (Based on aggregate user location data).
- **AI Reviews**: Summarize hundreds of reviews into quick tags: *"Great Wi-Fi"*, *"Drafty"*, *"Good Coffee"*.

---

## Phase 2: The Intelligent Tutor (Academic)
*Focus: Removing friction from learning.*

### 3. Smart Scheduling Assistant
Eliminate the "When are you free?" back-and-forth.
- **Auto-Suggest**: AI looks at both the Student and Tutor's calendars (if connected) and suggests the optimal 3 slots.
- **Conflict Resolution**: "You usually have lunch at 12:00, let's suggest 1:00 PM instead."

### 4. "NerdBot" Tutor Helper
A lightweight bot in the chat between Student and Tutor.
- **Pre-Session Prep**: Asks the student specific questions about their problem topic *before* the session starts, so the Tutor is prepared.
- **Resource Fetcher**: Can instantly pull up relevant formulas or diagrams into the chat.

---

## Phase 3: Viral & Engagement (Growth)
*Focus: Making the app fun and shareable.*

### 5. Nerd Herd "Wrapped" (Weekly/Semester)
Create FOMO and shareability.
- **Stats**: "You spent **42 hours** at the *Science Lab* this semester."
- **Social**: "You're in the top 5% of 'Early Birds' on campus."
- **Shareable Cards**: Beautiful, Instagram-story ready graphics generated by AI.

### 6. Gamified "King of the Hill"
- **Spot Dominance**: Verify you are at a spot to claim "Mayor" status.
- **Reward**: "Mayors" get boosted visibility or small discounts at campus cafes (future partnership potential).

### 7. Global "Nerd Feed"
- **Campus Pulse**: AI-curated feed of what's happening *now* academically on campus. *"Everyone is studying for CHEM101 finals at the Union."*
