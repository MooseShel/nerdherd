*Patent-Pending Logic for Verifiable Skill Tracking*

The Problem: A grade on a transcript doesn't prove you can communicate or lead. LinkedIn endorsements are fluff.
**The Solution**: Nerd Herd analyzes the *actual interactions* of learning to generate a trusted "Competence Score".

### How it Works (The Algorithm)
1.  **Semantic Interaction Analysis (NLP)**:
    *   Our AI doesn't just read "Thanks"; it analyzes the *explanation* given by a tutor.
    *   *Metric*: **Clarity Score**. Did the explanation use analogies? Was it Socratic?
2.  **Recursive Authority (The "PageRank" for People)**:
    *   If Student A tutors Student B, and Student B improves (or successfully tutors Student C), Student A gets a **"Root Teacher" bonus**.
    *   *Metric*: **Influence Depth**. How far did your knowledge travel?
3.  **The "Deep Work" Oracle (Geolocation + Behavior)**:
    *   Verifies actual study hours at verified locations (Libraries, Labs), distinguishing between "hanging out" vs "focused work" based on phone movement/usage patterns (with permission).
